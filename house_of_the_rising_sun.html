[Outro]
 
Strum each of these chords once while the organ plays: D F Am E Am E
 
The timing is quarter, eighth, eighth, eighth, quarter, quarter, quarter for each chord.
You can listen to a recording at http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IvI7h6pqBhA.  If you have
any questions, e-mail me.
