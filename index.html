Built by Musicians, for Musicians

The Sheet Music Directory was created and maintained by Mattia Lobrano, combining a passion for music with clean, modern web development. This is an independent project focused on creating the best possible experience for musicians worldwide.

The goal is simple: keep it clean, keep it fast, and keep it useful for everyone who loves to play.
